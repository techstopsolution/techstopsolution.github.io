About - Quant Quality

Quant Quality LLC was invented by highly experienced quality professionals who have working exposure to the automotive industries [TESLA, GM, FORD, FCA, BMW] for Quality Assurance, Process Improvement, Operational Excellence & Customer Satisfaction.

We are a global provider of quality assurances including sorting, inspection, rework, repair, New Product Launch & on-site quality representation in Automotive, Electronics, and other industries.

Quant itself stands for quantitative analysis; we are experts in collecting and analyzing inspection report data which can be used for root cause analysis to solve complex manufacturing problems quickly. Our web and mobile applications are designed to collect data with accuracy and automation by using the best technology such as Optical Character Recognition [OCR], Artificial Intelligence [AI], Machine Learning [ML], Deep Learning [DL], and Data Validation.

Quant Quality LLC is registered in SPARKS, Nevada USA, and follows a set of principles based on our unique SPARKS core principle:

S [Safety]: Safety first to protect yourself and our environment.

P [People]: Prioritize the people working inside your manufacturing unit, your investors, and your eventual customers.

A [Accuracy]: Aim for zero defects or errors. Our web and mobile app technology help to achieve higher accuracy.

R [Rate]: Commitment to operational excellence, intelligent planning, and focused effort resulting in a higher productivity rate.

K [Kaizen]: Excellence is not a destination; it is a continuous journey that never ends.

S [Satisfaction]: We satisfy you, so you can satisfy your customers. SPARK= Satisfaction

We at Quant Quality LLC also aim to provide our customers with world-class products and services to augment the growth of their organization. Quant Quality is a customer and performance-driven organization, which is why your feedback, growth, and performance are of utmost importance to the provision of unparalleled services.
