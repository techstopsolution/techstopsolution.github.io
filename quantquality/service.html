Gauge & Torque checks

Rework | Repair

We provide the rework solutions for defective parts

 
 
 

Our team is experienced in reworking rusty parts; We use formulated chemicals to remove the rust from parts quickly.

We also support reworking your assembly to meet torque specifications by 100% torque wrench or digital torque nut runner tool.

Our team is trained to use manual or electrical deburring tools to remove burr & excessive material.

We also do repair work for assembly units by replacing the NOK components from assembly and installing back OK components to assembly.

Containment | CS1 | CS2

Containment

The systematic search and quarantine of potentially nonconforming product/material throughout the supply chain and subsequent delivery of known conforming product/material. Aim is to send Zero Defects to your customer.

CS1 - Controlled Shipping Level 1

Our experts team is ready to support CS1 activities at your production line. Customized CS1 report will provide the data to narrow down the root cause quickly.

CS 2- Controlled Shipping Level 2

We perform CS2 activities at your warehouse/ customer site to contain nonconforming products.
