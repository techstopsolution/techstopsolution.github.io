LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY ELECTION - SIKKIM 2019

First time ever a branding company managed brand reputation of an Indian governance.

Having held the position of the chief minister of Sikkim for five consecutive terms, from 1994 to 2019, Shri Pawan Kumar Chamling holds the record of being India’s longest-serving chief minister.

In the present era of electronic revolution when the social media has become the means of all communication, That’s because we are hired for social media management and ground campaigns for Sikkim Democratic Front (SDF).

Jutyo Sikkim, Badhyo Sikkim

This campaign was dedicated to those who believe in Shri Pawan Chamling's vision of a Sikkim, and his prosperity, progress, and empowerment for all is a reality. SDF'S ideologies were to connect his ideology to each and every gender and provoke their past achievements.
