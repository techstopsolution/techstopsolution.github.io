VISUAL IDENTITY AND PACKAGING RANGE FOR COLD PRESSED OIL OR WOOD PRESSED OIL

Design of the brand identity framework, packaging design, visual design, and in-store kits for the coking and essential oils.

Angle have a psychological impact on people. The emotion generated from degree of angle is directly tied into the vision of company. 45 degree angle show the growth in equal way which is save energy, save planet with less expenses. minimlism use one of the most used principle “45 degrees the ideal angle to throw something” to achieve maximum potential- Somthing is light beam.

With the addition of organic, natural and free-form shapes. Wavy colorfull fills, dynamic curves, and powerful arches stand in contrast to the geometric, more functional style, creating unique, engaging and attention-grabbing compositions for LightGraph brand. minimlism focus on the light bean nature principle.
