VISUAL IDENTITY AND PACKAGING RANGE FOR COLD PRESSED OIL OR WOOD PRESSED OIL

Design of the brand identity framework, packaging design, visual design, and in-store kits for the coking and essential oils.

Brief

The company enables brands with loyal customers, who want to maintain a balance between physical, mental and spiritual practices to help in our self-realization. We discussed their yoga journey with the quote “Nature sets the example we should follow in our self-discovery journey.”

Here, minimlism needs to work along with yoga teachers and their students. We need to find the motivation behind yoga participants how they feel before and after.

After the study, we notice that they simply maintaining a balanced between inner peace and stress in running professional and personal life.

Concept

After discussion with client, minimlism took some inspiration from nature. we need to try lot of things that represnt balance concept i.e. Sun, Leaf, Roots, Water, Pebbels, Flowers, fire etc.

The pebble, carried by balanced approach, symbolizes multiple things.

It will prove to be a source of comfort, anguish, and relief to everybody.

We’re living in diversity, just like the beauty of a pebbly lies in the variety of the color, texture, and shape of its rocks and pebbles. Stand out from the crowd and celebrate uniqueness.
