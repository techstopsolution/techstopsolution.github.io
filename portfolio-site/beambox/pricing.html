There's no limits on how many people you can engage with through Beambox, though we do let users unsubscribe to maintain compliance.

How easy is it to install the Beambox?

It's plug and play, just connect Beambox to your existing router with the included ethernet cable and you're good to go.

Yes, almost 24/7! You can contact support at any time through Mission Control or via the form on the Support page of our website.
